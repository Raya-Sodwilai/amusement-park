Rider Height Requirements!

Height requirements are imposed by ride manufacturers and are strictly enforced by Amusements of America personnel. Below is a comprehensive list of our ride and attraction inventory and their respective height requirements and special restrictions. We recommend that you review this list before attending one of our events to help determine on what rides your child meets the posted height requirement.

Family Swinger

  • 3.5ft in min on outside seats, 3ft min on inside seats

Ferris Wheel

  • 4ft minimum

Rockin Tug

  • 3.5ft minimum

Sky Ride

  • 3.5ft minimum

Monkey Mayhem

  • 3.5ft min unless accompanied by an adult
